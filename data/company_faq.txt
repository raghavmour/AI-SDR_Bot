CRM Overview:
Our CRM platform helps sales teams manage leads, track interactions, and automate follow-ups. It integrates with tools like Slack, Gmail, Zoom, and Zapier.

Pricing:
We offer 3 plans:
- Starter: $29/month (up to 10 users)
- Team: $79/month (up to 50 users)
- Enterprise: Custom pricing

Features:
- Lead scoring and qualification
- Workflow automation
- Email tracking and templates
- Custom dashboards and reporting

Refund Policy:
Customers can request a full refund within 14 days of purchase. Refunds are not available after that period.

Security & Compliance:
Our platform is GDPR compliant and offers two-factor authentication (2FA), role-based access control, and encrypted data storage.

Support:
We provide 24/7 support via live chat and email. Phone support is available to Team and Enterprise users.

Demo & Onboarding:
You can schedule a demo directly from our website or request onboarding assistance after signing up.

Integrations:
Our CRM integrates with over 200+ apps, including Salesforce, HubSpot, Google Calendar, Mailchimp, and Stripe.

Analytics:
Real-time sales reports, conversion rates, and team performance dashboards are included in all plans.
